This Vancouver hotel was ranked #2 in Canada for 2021

Known for its attentive staff, a luxurious spa, and a high-end restaurant, the Rosewood Hotel Georgia offers a posh stay in the heart of downtown Vancouver and placed second overall. Report authors note how the Rosewood Hotel Georgia's "recent visitors said the staff's attention to detail makes guests feel like royalty." Unfortunately, the Rosewood Hotel Georgia has been closed since the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic in Vancouver, however, it is accepting reservations for May 1, 2021 and beyond now, perfect for anyone planning a spring staycation in the city.
